Minba

Academic excellence through study groups, tutoring, and scholastic competitions.

About the Division

What We Do

Minba is the academic division of SCOIS, dedicated to supporting every student's scholastic journey. We organize study groups, peer tutoring sessions, and represent the school in inter-school academic competitions. Our goal is to build a culture of learning and intellectual curiosity within the school community.

Humas

Building bridges between our school and the wider community through public relations.

About the Division

What We Do

Humas (Hubungan Masyarakat) is the public relations arm of SCOIS. We manage relationships with external organizations, coordinate outreach programs, and represent the school in inter-institutional events. Our team works to strengthen the school's presence in the wider community.

Tibkam

Ensuring a safe and orderly environment for all students and staff.

About the Division

What We Do

Tibkam (Ketertiban dan Keamanan) is responsible for maintaining discipline and security across school grounds. We coordinate with teachers and staff to enforce school rules, support event security, and foster a safe, respectful environment for everyone on campus.

Who We Are

More than a council — a community.

SCOIS (Student Council of Imam Syafii) is the official student representative body of our school, dedicated to fostering leadership, academic excellence, and a strong sense of community among all students.

We bridge the gap between students and faculty, organizing programs that enrich student life and create lasting memories.
